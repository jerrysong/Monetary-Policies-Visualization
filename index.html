Monetary Policy and Its Effects

"Give me control of a nation's money and I care not who makes its laws"
-- Mayer Amschel Bauer Rothschild

How central bank affects stock market

  • Directly buying them
  • Lowering the return on fixed investments, making investors more likely to enter stock market in hopes of finding better returns
  • Cheaper interest rates lead companies to borrow more money, which in turn they may use to buy back stocks

How central bank affects bond market

  • Interest rate changes directly move bond rates
  • By directly buying billions (or trillions!) of federal government bonds
  • By making future policy predictions (policy predictions can actually have more effect than policy changes)

How central bank affects house price
